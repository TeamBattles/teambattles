Subscribe to matches to receive updates even if you're not participating.

## How Subscriptions Work

Each subscription stores a small record that links you to a match and tracks how the subscription was created. The record has the following shape:

| Field       | Description                                                              |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| `matchId`   | The match you are subscribed to                                          |
| `userId`    | Your user account                                                        |
| `source`    | `ROSTER` (auto-subscribed) or `MANUAL` (you clicked Subscribe)           |
| `createdAt` | Timestamp of when the subscription was created                           |

The `source` value lets the app distinguish subscriptions you opted into manually from ones created automatically when you were added to a roster.

## Automatic Subscriptions (source: ROSTER)

You are automatically subscribed - with `source: "ROSTER"` - when you are added to a match roster, whether as active or bench. This is how participants stay informed without needing to opt in.

## Manual Subscriptions (source: MANUAL)

To follow a match you are not part of:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Open the match page">
		Navigate to any match you want to follow.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Click Subscribe">
		Click the **Subscribe** button. Your subscription is stored with `source: "MANUAL"`.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Receive updates">
		You will start receiving notifications for that match.
	</Step>
</Steps>

## What Subscribers Receive

- Score updates
- Status changes
- Match completion
- Dispute notifications

## Unsubscribing

To stop receiving notifications:

1. Go to the match page
2. Click **Unsubscribe**

<Note>
	Unsubscribing from a match you're participating in still sends you critical notifications (like
	when it's your turn to confirm scores).
</Note>

## Use Cases

**Following a tournament:**
Subscribe to matches in a tournament you're interested in.

**Watching friends:**
Subscribe to your friends' matches to follow their progress.

**Scouting opponents:**
Subscribe to potential opponents' matches to learn their strategies.
