Grow the TeamBattles community by referring friends. When someone you referred plays 5 matches, you earn 7 days of Premium trial.

## How It Works

1. Share your unique referral code, link, or QR code with friends
2. They sign up for TeamBattles using your referral
3. Once they play **5 matches**, you earn **7 Premium trial days**
4. Once they play 50+ matches, they "graduate" and free up a referral slot

## Finding Your Referral Code

<Steps>
	<Step title="Open Referrals">Navigate to the **Referrals** page from the sidebar</Step>
	<Step title="View Your Code">Your unique referral code is displayed at the top of the page</Step>
	<Step title="Share It">Use the code, link, or QR code to share with friends</Step>
</Steps>

## Ways to Share

| Method      | Description                                                            |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Code**    | Share your referral code directly - new users enter it during setup   |
| **Link**    | Copy a shareable URL in the format `https://teambattles.gg/ref/[code]` |
| **QR Code** | Display or download a QR code for in-person sharing                    |

<Tip>
	When someone visits your referral link, a tracking cookie is stored in their browser for 30 days.
	If they sign up within that window, the referral is automatically attributed to you.
</Tip>

## Premium Trial Rewards

You earn **7 Premium trial days** the first time each referred user reaches **5 matches played**. This is the only reward milestone - sign-up and earlier matches do not grant days.

- **Stacking**: Trial days accumulate across multiple referrals, up to a **90-day** cap.
- **Frozen while subscribed**: While you have an active, trialing, or cancelling paid subscription, your trial days are paused (your paid tier already grants the benefits).
- **Consumed while unsubscribed**: When you have no paid subscription, your trial days count down and grant you the Premium tier until they run out.
- **Permanent**: Granted days are never revoked - even if the referred user later churns or graduates.

<Note>You receive a notification when one of your referrals reaches 5 matches and grants you trial days.</Note>

## Referral Cap and Graduation

You can have up to **25 active referrals** at a time. Once a referred user plays **50 or more matches**, they "graduate" and free up an active referral slot.

- Graduated referrals still appear in your dashboard and count toward your stats
- Graduation happens automatically - no action needed from you
- Once a slot opens, you can refer someone new
- If you are at the 25-active cap, new 5-match milestones do not grant additional days until a slot frees up

## Viewing Your Referral Dashboard

The Referrals page shows a complete overview of your referral activity:

- **Summary cards** - Total referrals, active referrals, Premium days earned, graduation count
- **Referral list** - All referred users with their current status and match progress
- **Sharing tools** - Your code, link, and QR code for easy access

## Tracking Your Referral Progress

If you were referred by another user, a progress section appears at the bottom of the Referrals page. This shows:

- **Who referred you** - Your referrer's avatar and username
- **Match progress** - A progress bar tracking your matches played toward the 50-match graduation threshold
- **Graduated status** - Once you play 50+ matches, the section updates to show that you've completed your referral journey

<Tip>
	Playing your first 5 matches grants your referrer Premium trial days. Keep playing to help them out!
</Tip>

## Entering a Referral Code

If someone referred you to TeamBattles:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Sign Up">Create your account using any login provider</Step>
	<Step title="Profile Setup">During the setup flow, look for the referral code field</Step>
	<Step title="Enter Code">
		Type in the referral code you received (or it may be pre-filled if you used a referral link)
	</Step>
	<Step title="Complete Setup">Finish setting up your profile</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
	Referral codes can only be entered during initial profile setup. If you skip this step, you cannot
	add a referral code later.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

**"My referral link didn't track the signup"**

- The tracking cookie expires after 30 days
- The user may have cleared their cookies before signing up
- The user may have signed up on a different browser or device

**"I can't add more referrals"**

- You can have up to 25 active referrals at a time
- Wait for existing referrals to graduate (50+ matches played) to free up slots

**"I didn't receive my Premium trial days"**

- Days are granted only when a referral reaches 5 matches played for the first time
- If you are at the 25-active-referral cap, new milestones do not grant days until a slot frees up
- Your trial days are frozen while you have a paid subscription; they resume counting when you unsubscribe
- Check your referral dashboard for the current status
