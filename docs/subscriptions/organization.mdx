title: "Organization Assignment"
description: "Assign your subscription benefits to an organization"
---

Your subscription unlocks organization-level benefits like higher member limits, more team slots, and feature access. To apply these benefits, you must assign your subscription to a specific organization.

## How Assignment Works

- Each user can assign their subscription to **one organization** at a time
- The organization receives the benefits of your subscription tier
- Multiple users can assign their subscriptions to the same organization
- The organization's effective tier is the **highest** among all assigned subscriptions

## Assigning Your Subscription

<Steps>
	<Step title="Subscribe">Purchase a subscription from the Subscribe page</Step>
	<Step title="Choose Organization">
		After purchase, select which organization receives the benefits
	</Step>
	<Step title="Confirm">Confirm the assignment (this starts a cooldown period)</Step>
</Steps>

You can also assign or reassign from **Settings > Billing**.

## Cooldown Period

After assigning your subscription to an organization, there is a **7-day cooldown** before you can reassign it to a different organization. This prevents abuse of the system.

<Note>
	The cooldown only applies when changing organizations. Your first assignment has no cooldown.
</Note>

## Effective Tier

If multiple members assign their subscriptions to the same organization, the organization receives the benefits of the **highest** tier among all assignments.

**Example**: If one member has Basic and another has Premium, the organization operates at Premium tier.

## Organization Benefits by Tier

| Benefit            | Free | Basic | Standard | Premium |
| ------------------ | ---- | ----- | -------- | ------- |
| Members            | 20   | 50    | 100      | 200     |
| Teams              | 2    | 4     | 8        | 10      |
| League Teams       | 1    | 1     | 1        | 2       |
| Discovery Listing  | No   | Yes   | Yes      | Yes     |
| Private Visibility | No   | Yes   | Yes      | Yes     |

## What Happens When You Unsubscribe

If you cancel your subscription or it expires:

- Your organization falls back to the next highest assigned tier, or Free if none remain
- Existing members, teams, and data are preserved
- You cannot add new items beyond the Free tier limits until a subscription is reassigned
