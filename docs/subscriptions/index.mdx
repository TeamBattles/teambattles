TeamBattles offers optional subscription tiers that unlock additional features, higher limits, and faster benefit-action cooldowns. There is no in-app currency - every paid tier is a recurring real-money plan.

## Tier Comparison

| ------------------------------------ | -------- | ---------------- | ------------------- | ------------------- |
| **Organizations**                    | 1        | 3                | 5                   | 10                  |
| **Teams per Org**                    | 2        | 5                | 10                  | 20                  |
| **Org Members**                      | 20       | 50               | 100                 | 200                 |
| **Org Teams**                        | 2        | 4                | 8                   | 10                  |
| **Leagues per user**                 | 1        | 2                | 3                   | 5                   |
| **API Keys**                         | 2        | 3                | 5                   | 10                  |
| **Open Tickets**                     | 2        | 3                | 5                   | 10                  |
| **Saved strategies (Total)**         | 3        | Unlimited        | Unlimited           | Unlimited           |
| **Saved strategies (Per map)**       | Unlimited| 3                | 5                   | 10                  |
| **Custom maps**                      | 0        | 0                | 0                   | 50                  |
| **Strategy boards (per user)**[^1]   | 200      | 200              | 200                 | 200                 |
| **Discovery Listing**                | No       | Yes              | Yes                 | Yes                 |
| **Private Visibility**               | No       | Yes              | Yes                 | Yes                 |
| **OBS overlay watermark removal**    | No       | Yes              | Yes                 | Yes                 |
| **Strategy GIF export (no watermark)**| No      | Watermarked      | Yes                 | Yes                 |
| **Mid-Week Roster Update**           | No       | No               | No                  | Yes                 |

[^1]: Strategy boards are not tier-gated. The 200-board cap is a flat anti-abuse constant (`BOARD_CAP = 200` in `src/convex/functions/strategies/boards.ts`), not a per-tier limit.

<Note>
	**Strategy save limits use dual-axis gating.** On the **default (Free)** tier, `strategiesPerMap` is unlimited and `strategiesTotal` caps you at 3 saved strategies across all maps. On any **paid tier**, `strategiesTotal` is unlimited and `strategiesPerMap` caps how many strategies you can save per map (3 on Basic, 5 on Standard, 10 on Premium). The two limits never apply at the same time.
</Note>

## Benefit Actions

Some actions are not unlocked outright - your tier sets how **often** you can perform them. Each action has a per-entity cooldown that is anchored permanently, so cancelling and resubscribing never refreshes a cooldown.

| Benefit                       | Scopes            | Free          | Basic           | Standard        | Premium         |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------- | --------------- | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Change custom URL**         | User, Org, Team   | Locked        | 1x / week       | 1x / week       | 1x / week       |
| **Rename**                    | User, Org, Team   | 1x / 6 months | 1x / month      | 1x / month      | 1x / month      |
| **Reset Stats**               | User, Team, Org   | Locked        | 1x / 3 months   | 1x / 2 months   | 1x / month      |
| **Remove Donut & Breadstick** | User              | Locked        | 1x / month      | 1x / 2 weeks    | 1x / week       |
| **Feature org listing**       | Org               | Locked        | 1x / month (1 day) | 1x / month (3 days) | 1x / month (7 days) |

<Note>
	Resetting an organization's stats also resets all of its teams. The cooldown is anchored on the organization, not on the individual teams.
</Note>

## How Subscriptions Work

- **User-level**: Each subscription belongs to an individual user.
- **Org assignment**: You assign your subscription to one organization to unlock org-level benefits.
- **Effective tier**: An organization's tier is the highest among all subscriptions assigned to it; a team inherits its parent org's tier.
- **Soft limits**: If you downgrade, existing items above the new limit are preserved but you cannot add more.
- **Subscription badge**: Your tier is displayed on your public profile (can be hidden in billing settings).
- **Referral trials**: Referring active players grants Premium trial days (see [Referrals](/referrals)).

## The Subscription Sheet

Every subscription moment - a locked feature, an action on cooldown, a plan change, or a limit reached - opens one universal sheet. The sheet explains why the action is unavailable (for example, "available again in 9 days, or upgrade for weekly"), shows the tier you need, and routes you to Polar hosted checkout to subscribe or change plans. On desktop the sheet slides in from the right; on mobile it slides up from the bottom.

## A la Carte Purchases

A small set of one-time, real-money items can be purchased directly (fulfilled through Polar). Today the only such item is **Gift D&B Removal**, which clears another user's Donut and Breadstick counts. You cannot gift it to yourself.
