title: "Billing"

---

Access billing settings from **Settings > Billing** to manage your subscription, view payment history, and configure your subscription badge.

## Current Plan

Your billing page shows your current subscription tier, including:

- Tier name and price for your billing interval (monthly or yearly)
- Subscription status (active, trialing, cancels at period end)
- Next billing date

While you are on a free trial, the plan card shows a "No charge until {date}" message in place of the next billing date - you are not charged until the trial ends.

## Subscription Badge

Your subscription tier is displayed as a badge on your public profile. You can control this visibility:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">Navigate to Settings > Billing</Step>
  <Step title="Find Badge Toggle">Look for the "Show subscription badge" toggle</Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Visibility">Turn it off to hide the badge from your public profile</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
	When hidden, your profile will appear as if you have the Free tier to other users. Your actual
	subscription benefits remain active.
</Note>

## Payment History

The billing page includes a payment history table showing:

| Column      | Description                    |
| ----------- | ------------------------------ |
| Date        | When the payment was processed |
| Description | What was purchased             |
| Amount      | Payment amount                 |
| Status      | Paid or Refunded               |

Subscription charges and one-time a la carte purchases both appear here. Each description is sourced from the product catalog display name (for example, "Premium (Annual)" or "Remove Donut & Breadstick (Gift)").

## Managing Your Subscription

To upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription, open the subscription sheet from the billing page or any locked feature:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Open the Sheet">Click Upgrade, Change Plan, or any locked feature</Step>
	<Step title="Choose a Tier">Select the tier you want</Step>
	<Step title="Complete Payment">You are routed to Polar hosted checkout</Step>
	<Step title="Benefits Apply">New limits and features apply immediately</Step>
</Steps>

To manage payment methods or cancel directly, use the **Manage subscription** button. It opens your Polar customer portal in a new tab, and the portal's `return_url` brings you back to **Settings > Billing** when you are done.

<Warning>
	Downgrading preserves existing items above the new limit, but you will not be able to add more
	until you are within the new limit.
</Warning>
