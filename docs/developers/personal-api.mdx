---
title: Personal API
description: Act as yourself - your matches, account connections, and game ranks - with a personal API key.
---

The **Personal API** lets a key act as its owner over their own data: your matches and
ongoing matches, your account connections and game ranks, and match chat. It requires a
**personal** key; write actions require a paid (Pro or higher) developer plan.

How many personal keys you can create scales with your **consumer account subscription**,
not your developer plan - the developer plan governs developer-app keys, webhooks, and
write access instead. See
[Plans and feature gates](/developers/authentication#plans-and-feature-gates).
