Sign up using one of these providers:

- **Discord**
- **Twitch**
- **GitHub**
- **Google**
- **Battle.net**

Click your preferred provider and authorize TeamBattles to access your account.

## Set Up Your Profile

{/_
IMAGE NEEDED: Profile setup wizard
Type: screenshot
Shows: Profile setup form after first login
Notes: Show required fields highlighted
_/}

After your first login, a two-step setup wizard guides you through getting started:

**Step 1: Profile Setup**

Fill in your required details:

- **Display name** (1-50 characters)
- **Username** (3-30 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores)

You can also add optional info like bio, pronouns, birthday, country, timezone, languages, and profile images.

**Step 2: Choose Your Path**

After completing your profile, you'll see a "What would you like to do?" screen with four options:

- **Create Organization** - Start your own org
- **Join Organization** - Find an existing org to join
- **Join League** - Browse competitive leagues
- **Create Team** - Form a team (requires org membership)

Selecting an option starts an [interactive tutorial](/tutorials) that walks you through the process with highlighted UI elements and step-by-step instructions.

<Tip>You can skip either step and complete things later. Tutorials are always available from your [dashboard](/dashboard).</Tip>

## Connect Your Gaming Platforms

To participate in matches, link your gaming accounts:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Settings">Click your avatar and select Settings</Step>
	<Step title="Open Connections">Navigate to the Connections tab</Step>
	<Step title="Link Platforms">Click "Connect" next to each platform you use</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
	**Activision connection is required** for Call of Duty matches. All team members must have this
	linked.
</Warning>

## Create or Join an Organization

Organizations are groups that manage teams. You need to be in an organization before creating a team.

**Option A: Create an organization**

1. Click "Create Organization" from your dashboard
2. Enter a name (3-50 characters) and tag (2-6 characters)
3. Set visibility to public or private

**Option B: Join an existing organization**

1. Go to Discovery to browse public organizations
2. Click "Request to Join" on an organization
3. Wait for an admin to approve your request

## Create a Team

Once you're in an organization:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to your organization">Click on your organization from the dashboard</Step>
	<Step title="Create a team">Click "Create Team" (requires Admin or Owner role)</Step>
	<Step title="Configure the team">Set the name, tag, game, game mode, platform, and region</Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Match

With a team ready:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Matches">Click "Create Match" from your dashboard or team page</Step>
	<Step title="Select your team">Choose which team is playing</Step>
	<Step title="Configure the match">
		Set game mode, series length, map selection type, and scheduling
	</Step>
	<Step title="Select your roster">Choose active players and optionally set bench players</Step>
	<Step title="Publish">Click "Publish" to make the match visible to other teams</Step>
</Steps>
