Customize your chat experience with notification settings.

## Accessing Settings

Click the **gear icon** in the chat header to open settings.

## Sound Notifications

Enable audio alerts for new messages:

- Different sounds for teammate vs opponent messages
- Disabled by default

**To enable:**

1. Open Chat Settings
2. Toggle **Sound Notifications** on
3. Choose sound preferences

## Browser Notifications

Receive notifications even when not on the page:

- Requires browser permission
- Disabled by default

**To enable:**

1. Open Chat Settings
2. Toggle **Browser Notifications** on
3. Grant permission when prompted

<Note>
	If you previously blocked notifications, you'll need to enable them in your browser settings.
</Note>

### Enabling in Browser Settings

If blocked, instructions appear in Chat Settings explaining how to:

1. Open browser settings
2. Find site permissions
3. Allow notifications for TeamBattles

## Unread Badge

- Shows count of unread messages across all rooms
- Appears on the chat button
- Resets when you open a room

## Position & Size (Desktop)

Your floating chat window preferences:

- Position on screen
- Window size

These are saved in your browser's local storage.

<Warning>
	Clearing browser data will reset chat preferences. Settings are device-specific and not synced.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

**"I'm not receiving notifications"**

- Check if sounds and browser notifications are enabled in Chat Settings
- For browser notifications, grant permission when prompted
- If blocked, check browser settings

**"My chat position/size isn't saved"**

- Settings are stored in local storage
- Clearing browser data resets them
- Settings are device-specific, not synced

**"I can't upload images"**

- Your team may have reached the 10 image limit
- Check that images are under 2 MB
- Try different formats (JPG, PNG, WebP)
