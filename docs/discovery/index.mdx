Browse public organizations to find gaming communities.

## Finding Organizations

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Discovery">Navigate to the Discovery section</Step>
	<Step title="Search">Search by organization name</Step>
	<Step title="Browse Cards">
		View organization cards showing: - Name and tag - Avatar and banner - Member count - Win
		statistics
	</Step>
	<Step title="View Profile">Click to see full organization profile</Step>
	<Step title="Request to Join">Click "Request to Join" for public organizations</Step>
</Steps>

## Organization Cards

Each card displays:

- **Name & Tag** - Organization identity
- **Avatar** - Organization logo
- **Members** - Total member count
- **Stats** - Win/loss record
- **Verified Badge** - If officially verified

## Requesting to Join

For public organizations:

1. Click "Request to Join" on the organization card or profile
2. Wait for an admin to review your request
3. You'll receive a notification when approved or denied

<Note>Private organizations don't appear in Discovery. You need a direct invite.</Note>

## After Joining

Once approved:

- You become a Member of the organization
- You can join teams within the organization
- You appear in the organization's member list
