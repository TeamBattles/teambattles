Browse available matches and find opponents for your team.

<Note>
	Browsing available matches is public - you can open the Matches section and view published matches
	without an account. You only need to sign in (with a Captain or Co-Captain role) to accept a match.
</Note>

## Finding Matches

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Matches">Navigate to the Matches section</Step>
	<Step title="Browse Available">View the "Available Matches" section</Step>
	<Step title="Filter">Filter by: - Game - Status - Platform - Region</Step>
	<Step title="Find a Match">
		Look for matches that fit your team's skill level and preferences
	</Step>
</Steps>

## Match Cards

Each card shows:

- **Game & Mode** - What's being played
- **Teams** - Creator's team info
- **Series** - Best of 1, 3, 5, or 7
- **Schedule** - Play Now or scheduled time
- **Requirements** - Platform, input restrictions

## Filtering Options

| Filter   | Options                          |
| -------- | -------------------------------- |
| Game     | Call of Duty, etc.               |
| Status   | Available, Pending, etc.         |
| Platform | PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Crossplay |
| Region   | NA East, EU, Asia, etc.          |

## Accepting a Match

1. Find a match that fits your team
2. Click **Accept**
3. Select your team and roster
4. Submit your acceptance request
5. Wait for the creator to approve

<Tip>You can accept multiple matches simultaneously. Creators choose which team to play.</Tip>

## Requirements

To accept a match:

- Your team must be for the same game
- You must be Captain or Co-Captain
- Your team must meet platform/input requirements
- Players need required gaming connections
