Access settings by clicking your avatar and selecting **Settings**.

## General Settings

| Setting            | Description              | Limit                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Display Name       | Your public name         | 1-50 characters        |
| Email              | Contact email            | -                      |
| Bio                | About yourself           | 500 characters         |
| Profile Visibility | Who can see your profile | Public/Limited/Private |
| Timezone           | Your local time          | -                      |
| Language           | Interface language       | 12 languages (see below) |

## Changing Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings > General**
2. Update your information
3. Click **Save**

## Language Settings

TeamBattles is available in 12 languages:

- English (default)
- Spanish (Español)
- Polish (Polski)
- German (Deutsch)
- French (Français)
- Italian (Italiano)
- Korean (한국어)
- Chinese, Simplified (中文)
- Portuguese, Brazil (Português)
- Japanese (日本語)
- Russian (Русский)
- Turkish (Türkçe)

On your first visit we detect your browser's preferred language automatically and show the interface in that language when it's supported, falling back to English otherwise.

To change it yourself:

1. Go to **Settings > Appearance**
2. Find the **Language** section
3. Select your preferred language
4. The interface updates immediately, and your choice is remembered on this device

## Session Information

- Sessions last **7 days** before requiring re-login
- Logging in on a new device doesn't log you out elsewhere
