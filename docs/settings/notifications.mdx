Choose which notifications you want to receive and how you get them.

## Accessing Notification Preferences

1. Click your avatar and select **Settings**
2. Go to **Notifications**

## Notification Channels

| Channel | Status      | Description                      |
| ------- | ----------- | -------------------------------- |
| In-App  | Active      | Notifications inside the app     |
| Email   | Coming soon | Notifications sent to your email |
| Push    | Coming soon | Browser push notifications       |

<Info>
	Only the in-app channel is currently active. Email and push channels will be available in a future
	update.
</Info>

## Notification Categories

Notifications are organized into categories. You can toggle an entire category on or off, or expand it to control individual notification types.

### Match (18 types)

Notifications related to your matches:

- Challenge accepted
- Match updated / starting soon / completed
- Match cancelled / deleted / unpublished
- Match request received / approved / declined
- Score submitted / needs review / confirmed
- Lobby code updated
- Attendance changed
- Dispute raised / resolved
- Added to match roster

### Organization (8 types)

Activity within your organizations:

- Invite received
- Join request received / approved / declined
- Member accepted / kicked / left
- Ownership transferred

### Team (9 types)

Activity within your teams:

- Invite received
- Join request received / approved / declined
- Member accepted / kicked / left
- Member promoted / demoted

### Ticket (7 types)

Updates on your support tickets:

- Ticket created / closed
- New reply
- Status changed
- Ticket assigned
- Member added / removed

### Payment (2 types)

Billing and subscription updates:

- Payment completed
- Subscription activated

<Warning>
	Both payment notifications are **critical** and cannot be disabled. You will always receive these
	notifications.
</Warning>

### League (3 types)

League participation updates:

- Application submitted
- Application approved / denied

### Developer (5 types)

Notifications for developer accounts:

- Application submitted / approved / denied
- Account suspended
- Webhook auto-disabled

<Note>
	Developer notifications only appear if you have an approved developer account. The "Account
	Suspended" notification is **critical** and cannot be disabled.
</Note>

### Other (4 types)

General platform notifications:

- Trophy awarded
- Platform announcements
- Referral signup
- Referral milestone

## How Toggling Works

### Category Toggle

Each category has a **master toggle** at the top of its card. Turning it off disables all notification types in that category at once. Turning it back on restores your previous per-type settings.

### Individual Type Toggle

Expand a category to see its individual notification types. Toggle each one independently to fine-tune which notifications you receive.

### Critical Notifications

Some notifications are marked with a **lock icon** and cannot be disabled:

- **Payment Completed** - Confirms successful payments
- **Subscription Activated** - Confirms subscription changes
- **Developer Account Suspended** - Alerts you to account restrictions

These are always delivered to protect your account.
