Customize how TeamBattles looks.

## Theme

Choose between:

- **Onyx** - Default black interface
- **Navy** - Blue-black Arena interface
- **Graphite** - Neutral dark interface
- **Bone** - Bright interface
- **System** - Follow your device setting

### Changing Theme

1. Go to **Settings > Appearance**
2. Select your preferred theme
3. Changes apply immediately

## System Theme

TeamBattles can follow your system's theme:

- When your system switches to dark mode, TeamBattles follows
- When your system switches to light mode, TeamBattles follows

## Additional Options

Future customization options may include:

- Accent colors
- Font size preferences
- Animation preferences
