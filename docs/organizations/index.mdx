Organizations are groups that create and manage multiple teams. Think of them as esports clubs or gaming communities.

## What Organizations Do

- **Group players** - Bring together gamers with shared interests
- **Manage teams** - Create multiple teams for different games or skill levels
- **Control access** - Invite members and manage permissions
- **Track stats** - See combined statistics across all teams

Your organization profile displays:

- Name, tag, avatar, and banner
- Member count and team count
- Win/loss/draw statistics
- Social links (Twitter, YouTube, Twitch, Discord, Website)
- Verified badge (if verified)

<Note>
	Aggregate organization stats (wins, losses, draws, total matches, experience) only count teams
	that are currently active and not disabled. Archived or disabled teams are excluded from the
	totals shown on the profile.
</Note>

## Verified Organizations

Organizations can receive a verified badge indicating official recognition. Verification is granted by platform administrators.

<Tip>Contact support to request verification for established organizations.</Tip>

## Limits

| Setting | Limit                      |
| ------- | -------------------------- |
| Name    | 3-50 characters            |
| Tag     | 2-6 characters (uppercase) |
| Members | No limit                   |
| Teams   | No limit                   |
| Avatar  | 1 MB max                   |
| Banner  | 2 MB max                   |
