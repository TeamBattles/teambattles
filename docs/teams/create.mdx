Create a team to compete in matches. You must be an organization Owner or Admin.

## Steps to Create

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Your Organization">
		Navigate to the organization where you want to create the team
	</Step>
	<Step title="Click Create Team">Find the button in the Teams section</Step>
	<Step title="Enter Basic Info">
		- **Name** - Must be unique within your organization - **Tag** - 2-5 characters (becomes
		uppercase)
	</Step>
	<Step title="Select Game Settings">
		- **Game** - e.g., Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 - **Playlist** - Game mode (e.g., Hardpoint, Search
		& Destroy) - **Team Type** - Regular or League
	</Step>
	<Step title="Set Preferences">
		- **Platform** - PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Console Only, or Crossplay - **Region** - NA East, NA
		West, EU, Asia, etc.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Add Branding (Optional)">- Avatar (1 MB max) - Banner (2 MB max)</Step>
	<Step title="Create">Click "Create" to finish</Step>
</Steps>

## Platform Options

| Platform     | Description              |
| ------------ | ------------------------ |
| PC           | PC players only          |
| Xbox         | Xbox players only        |
| PlayStation  | PlayStation players only |
| Console Only | Xbox + PlayStation       |
| Crossplay    | All platforms            |

## Region Options

- None (no preference)
- NA East / NA West
- EU (Europe)
- Asia
- Oceania
- South America
- Middle East
- Africa

## Requirements

- You must be an **Owner** or **Admin** of the organization
- Team name must be unique within the organization
- Team tag must be unique within the organization

<Tip>Choose your game and playlist carefully - they cannot be changed after creation.</Tip>
