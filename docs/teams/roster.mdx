Teams have two roster tiers: active players who participate in matches, and bench players as backups.

## Active Roster

Players who will participate in matches:

- Selected when creating or accepting matches
- Must have required gaming connections linked
- Minimum 1 active player required per match

## Bench

Backup players with priority ordering:

- Can be swapped in if active players are unavailable
- Priority order determines who gets called up first
- Drag and drop to reorder priorities

	<Step title="Set Priority">Assign each bench player a priority number (1, 2, 3, etc.)</Step>
	<Step title="Call Up When Needed">
		If an active player can't attend, the #1 priority bench player gets called up
	</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Drag and drop bench players to reorder their priority.</Tip>

## Automatic Bench Promotion

When an active roster player sets their attendance to "Not Attending":

- The highest priority bench player is automatically promoted
- This ensures your team always has enough players
- You can manually adjust after auto-promotion

## Managing Your Roster

### Move a Player

1. Go to your team's **Roster** tab
2. Find the player
3. Click **Move to Bench** or **Move to Active**

### Remove a Player

1. Go to your team's **Roster** tab
2. Find the player
3. Click **Remove from Team**

### Invite a Player

1. Go to your team's **Members** tab
2. Click **Invite**
3. Search for an organization member
4. Send the invite

<Note>Players must be members of the parent organization before joining a team.</Note>
