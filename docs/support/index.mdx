Create support tickets for issues, report problems, and track resolution.

## Accessing Support

Access the Support page from:

- **User menu** - Click your avatar > Support
- **Direct URL** - Navigate to `/support`

## Ticket Status Lifecycle

| Status             | Meaning             | What's Next           |
| ------------------ | ------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Open**           | Newly created       | Awaiting staff review |
| **In Progress**    | Staff working on it | Ongoing investigation |
| **Awaiting Reply** | Staff responded     | You need to respond   |
| **Resolved**       | Issue addressed     | Confirm or reopen     |
| **Closed**         | Ticket completed    | No further action     |

## Viewing Your Tickets

The Support page shows all your tickets:

**Ticket List:**

- Ticket number and subject
- Status badge
- Category and linked entity
- Creation date and last activity
- Unread indicator for staff replies

**Filtering:**

- By status (All, Open, In Progress, etc.)
- By category (Account, Technical, etc.)

**Sorting:**

- Newest first (default)
- Oldest first
- Recently updated

## Quick Help: Discord

For quick questions or community support, join our Discord server. A link is always visible on the Support page.
