	<Step title="Submit">Send for opponent confirmation</Step>

## Score Confirmation

After you submit:

1. Opponent reviews the scores
2. If accurate, they **Confirm**
3. If incorrect, they **Dispute**
4. Once confirmed, stats are updated

<Warning>
	To submit or view scores you must be a **captain** of either the creator team or the accepted
	team for that match. Non-captains and members of other teams will see an
	`ONLY_CAPTAINS_CAN_SUBMIT_SCORES` error.
</Warning>

<Note>
	When a team overwrites the opposing team's score submission, a dispute notification is
	automatically posted in the match chat.
</Note>

## Dispute Resolution

When a dispute is filed:

- Match status changes to "Disputed"
- Admins review the evidence
- Resolution is determined
- Stats are updated based on the ruling

<Note>All screenshots are automatically scanned for NSFW content before uploading.</Note>

## Troubleshooting

**"I can't submit scores"**

- Only team captains can submit
- Match must be "In Progress" status
- Verify you have at least one active player

**"I can't start the match"**

- Both teams must have confirmed rosters
- Match must be in "Accepted" status
- Only captains can start matches
