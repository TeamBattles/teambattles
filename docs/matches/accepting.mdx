## Sending an Acceptance Request

<Steps>
	<Step title="Click Accept">On a match you want to play</Step>
	<Step title="Select Your Team">Choose which team is accepting</Step>
	<Step title="Select Roster">Pick your active players and bench</Step>
	<Step title="Submit">Your request is sent to the match creator</Step>
</Steps>

## Acceptance Status

| Status        | Meaning                     |
| ------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Pending**   | Awaiting creator's decision |
| **Approved**  | Creator accepted your team  |
| **Rejected**  | Creator declined            |
| **Withdrawn** | You cancelled your request  |

<Note>
	Match creators can receive multiple acceptance requests. They choose which team to play against.
</Note>

## Checking In

For scheduled matches:

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to Match Page">Navigate to the accepted match</Step>
	<Step title="Click Check In">When ready to play</Step>
	<Step title="Wait for Opponent">Both teams must check in</Step>
	<Step title="Match Ready">Status changes to "Ready" when both teams check in</Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

To accept a match, your team must:

- Be the same game as the match
- Have a Captain or Co-Captain accepting
- Meet platform/input device requirements
- Have players with required connections

## Troubleshooting

**"I can't accept this match"**

- Your team may not match the game
- You may not have the required role
- Platform/input requirements may not be met

**"My acceptance was rejected"**

- The creator chose a different team
- Try accepting other available matches
