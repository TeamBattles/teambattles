Our Security Policy explains how to responsibly report a security vulnerability you find in the
TeamBattles platform, what you can expect from us in return, and the protections we extend to
researchers who act in good faith.

**Key topics covered:**

- Scope: which systems are in and out of scope
- How to report a vulnerability and what to include
- Safe harbor for good-faith research
- Guidelines for researchers
- Out-of-scope and prohibited activities
- Our response commitments and timelines
- Coordinated disclosure
- Recognition for reporters
- Contact information

<Note>
	Report security issues privately to [security@teambattles.gg](mailto:security@teambattles.gg). The
	same address is published in our
	[security.txt](https://teambattles.gg/.well-known/security.txt) file. Please do not disclose
	vulnerabilities through public channels before they are resolved.
</Note>
