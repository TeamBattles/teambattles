Every league has a public profile page that displays its identity, standings, staff, and supported games.

## Profile Overview

The top of the profile shows the league's core identity:

- **Name** and **description**
- **Avatar** and **banner** images
- **Verified** badge (if verified by TeamBattles staff)
- **Featured** badge (if currently featured)
- **Status** indicator (active, suspended, etc.)

## Standings Preview

The profile includes a preview of the current standings, showing top teams ranked by points. You can click through to the full [leaderboard](/leagues/leaderboards) for detailed stats and season selection.

## Staff Section

The profile lists league staff members and their roles:

- **Owner** - The league creator or current owner
- **Admins, Managers, Members** - Supporting staff with various permissions

## Games

A league can support one or more games. The profile shows which games are active, and each game can have its own:

- Rules and match settings
- Seasons and standings
- Points configuration

## Social Links

Leagues can add social links to their profile, making it easy for teams to find the league's community channels (Discord, Twitter, YouTube, etc.).

<Tip>If you are interested in joining a league, check the social links to connect with the community before applying.</Tip>
