The **Discover** page lists all active leagues on TeamBattles. Each league card shows:

- League name, avatar, and description
- Supported games
- Verified badge (if applicable)
- Team count

Click any league card to visit its [profile page](/leagues/profiles).

## Search and Filters

Use the search bar to find leagues by name. You can also filter results by:

- **Game** - Show only leagues that support a specific game
- **Status** - Filter by active or featured leagues

<Tip>If you already know the league name, searching is the fastest way to find it.</Tip>

## Featured Leagues

Featured leagues appear prominently on the Discover page. League featuring is managed by TeamBattles staff and highlights leagues with active communities and well-run competitions.

Featured status is temporary and has an expiration date.

## Verified Leagues

Verified leagues display a verification badge on their profile and in search results. Verification indicates that TeamBattles staff has confirmed the league's legitimacy and quality.

<Note>Verification and featuring are separate - a league can be verified without being featured, and vice versa.</Note>
