title: "Managing Seasons"
description: "Create and manage competitive seasons for your league"
---

Seasons divide league competition into distinct time periods. Each season tracks its own standings, stats, and leaderboards independently.

## Season States

A season progresses through three states:

| State         | Description                                                                       |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **UPCOMING**  | The season has been created but is not yet active. No stats are tracked.           |
| **ACTIVE**    | The season is live. Match results award points and update standings.               |
| **COMPLETED** | The season has ended. Standings are finalized and no new points are awarded.       |

<Note>Only one season per game can be active at a time. You must complete the current season before activating a new one.</Note>

<Steps>
	<Step title="Go to the Competition Tab">
		Open your league's admin panel and navigate to the **Competition** tab.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Select the Game">
		Choose which game this season is for using the game selector.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Click Create Season">
		Find the Seasons section and click the create button.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Enter Season Details">
		- **Name** - A descriptive name (e.g., "Season 1", "Summer 2026")
		- **Start date** - When the season is scheduled to begin
		- **End date** - When the season is scheduled to end
	</Step>
	<Step title="Save">
		The season is created in the **UPCOMING** state. It will not track any stats until activated.
	</Step>
</Steps>

## Activating a Season

When you are ready to start tracking stats, activate the season:

1. Find the season in the Competition tab
2. Click **Activate**
3. The season moves to **ACTIVE** and all future match results for that game will award points under this season

<Tip>You can create a season ahead of time and activate it later when you are ready to begin competition.</Tip>

Any matches played before a season is activated are tracked as **preseason** stats (see below).

## Completing a Season

When a season ends:

1. Navigate to the active season in the Competition tab
2. Click **Complete**
3. The season moves to **COMPLETED** and standings are finalized

After completion:

- The season's leaderboard becomes a permanent historical record
- No further points are awarded under this season
- You can create and activate a new season for the next period

## Preseason Stats

Matches played when no season is active are tracked as **preseason** stats. Preseason stats:

- Still award and deduct points using the league's points configuration
- Appear in the leaderboard under the "Preseason" option in the season selector
- Are tracked separately from named seasons - they do not carry over

<Note>Preseason is useful for letting teams warm up or test the league's rule settings before an official season begins.</Note>
